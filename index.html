Welcome to Namma Kudla

Namma Kudla, also known as Mangalore, is a vibrant coastal city located in the state of Karnataka, famous for its rich culture, delicious food, beautiful beaches, and friendly people. The word “Kudla” comes from the Tulu language, meaning “junction” or “meeting place,” symbolizing how the city connects people, traditions, and communities. Situated between the Arabian Sea on one side and the Western Ghats on the other, Kudla enjoys a unique mix of nature, modernity, and heritage. It is one of Karnataka’s fastest-growing educational, commercial, and cultural hubs.
